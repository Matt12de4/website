Ps4

                                                                                                                       
                         

I think everyone should at least play this game once in their lifetime. I really cannot put it into words how breathtakingly beautiful this game is. The spectacular scenery throughout the whole game is nothing but amazing, you really get blown away by how each part was done, It feels so futuristic. Then there's the storyline again the amount of depth and thought that was put into this goes beyond most Hollywood movies so much heart was put into this game, it deserves it own academy award. Like everyone else has said you choose your own path this will get your heart pounding trying not to make a mistake,it feels like watching Game of thrones, your favourite characters can die very fast and easily

                                  

Marvel's Spider-Man is one of the best games I have ever played, there are so many things that I enjoyed here that I wish I could play it all over again. It is a beautiful tribute to one of my favorite characters of all time. The way Insomniac crafted this game shows how passionate and dedicated the studio was to these characters and universe. The gameplay is very engaging. Even when you are doing nothing, it is insanely fun to swing around the New York skyscrapers. The controls are fun to use and in hard-mode, it provides a fun and stimulating experience.

                                      

Horizon Zero Dawn has, without a doubt, one of the most vast and detailed worlds built in a masterpiece of a game. Set in a post-apocalyptic world in the far future, one can see the immense effort Guerrilla Games has put into fleshing out the lore and intricacies of the Horizon universe. During my entire playthrough, what astonished me the most was the story. Every step of the way, I found myself stunned, appalled, excited, and even anxious, which made me ponder even more about what was coming next. Even minor pieces of lore, scattered across the map, provided a lot of insight into the story at large.
